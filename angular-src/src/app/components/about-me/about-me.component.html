Employee Managament

For a few years I have been working as a security guard, currently working at the President's Residence.
Working as a security guard means working shifts. Up until a few years ago employee shift management was done manually, employees would send the manager what shifts they can / can not work during a week and the manager tried to take the requests into consideration. If their are a small amount of guards then this would be an easy proccess to do manually, but if their are a lot of employees, manual shift managment can become very problematic. I've created my project to try to provide a management solution.
On this project I am working with NodeJS, Express, Mongodb, MLab, Angular and Heroku.

How It Works

Registration is solely for the employee.
To register you need to contact the manager.
After log-in there are several options and administrator notifications.
Options in Home Page:

  • Profile page
  • The Edit Personal Details page does not require updating your password.
  • Contact page: List of contacts in the system records. Mailing option is currently inactive.
  • Week page: Here the employee is shown the number of shifts he or she is required to open.
